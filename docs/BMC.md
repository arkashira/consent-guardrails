# Business Model Canvas: Consent-Guardrails

## Value Proposition
Consent-Guardrails provides a standardized, adaptable framework for managing consent and data protection in AI and technology systems. Our platform empowers government officials, policymakers, and advocacy groups with tools to ensure compliance with evolving data protection regulations while maintaining operational efficiency.

## Customer Segments
1. **Government Officials** – Public sector employees responsible for digital governance and regulatory compliance
2. **Policymakers** – Legislators and regulators crafting data protection legislation and guidelines
3. **Advocacy Groups** – Non-profit organizations focused on digital rights and privacy advocacy
4. **AI Developers** – Technology companies building AI systems requiring robust consent management

## Channels
1. **Direct Sales** – B2G partnerships with government agencies and regulatory bodies
2. **Public Procurement Platforms** – Government contract portals and digital procurement systems
3. **Industry Conferences** – Participation in digital governance and privacy conferences
4. **Open Source Community** – GitHub-based community engagement and contribution
5. **Consulting Partnerships** – Strategic alliances with policy consulting firms

## Revenue Streams
1. **Subscription Licensing** – Tiered pricing models based on organization size and feature requirements
2. **Professional Services** – Custom implementation and compliance consulting
3. **Training Programs** – Workshops and certification courses for government personnel
4. **API Access** – Pay-per-use API access for integration with existing systems
5. **Premium Support** – Dedicated support packages for high-risk or large-scale deployments

## Cost Structure
1. **Technology Development** – Ongoing R&D for framework updates and new features
2. **Platform Operations** – Infrastructure hosting, security, and maintenance costs
3. **Sales & Marketing** – Direct sales team, marketing campaigns, and partnership development
4. **Professional Services** – Consulting staff, training materials, and implementation support
5. **Regulatory Compliance** – Legal and compliance overhead for maintaining standards

## Key Resources
1. **Core Frameworks** – Verified, production-ready consent and data protection frameworks
2. **Knowledge Base** – Comprehensive repository of regulatory requirements and best practices
3. **Development Team** – Expert engineers and policy specialists maintaining the platform
4. **Partnership Network** – Strategic relationships with government agencies and advocacy organizations
5. **Data Assets** – Datasets from Axentx's training corpus relevant to consent management

## Key Activities
1. **Framework Development** – Continuous evolution of consent management frameworks
2. **Compliance Monitoring** – Tracking regulatory changes and updating frameworks accordingly
3. **Customer Onboarding** – Implementation and integration support for new users
4. **Community Engagement** – Maintaining open-source contributions and stakeholder communication
5. **Training Delivery** – Providing educational content and certification programs

## Key Partners
1. **Government Agencies** – Collaborative partnerships with public sector organizations
2. **Legal Experts** – Advisory relationships with privacy law specialists
3. **Technology Vendors** – Integration partners for system interoperability
4. **Academic Institutions** – Research collaborations for policy development
5. **Industry Associations** – Membership in digital governance and privacy organizations
