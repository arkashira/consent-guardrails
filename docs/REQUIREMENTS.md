## Functional Requirements
-------------------------

### FR-1: Consent Framework Standardization

*   The platform shall provide a standardized framework for consent and data protection in AI and technology.
*   The framework shall be adaptable to various industries and use cases.
*   The framework shall be easily integratable with existing systems and technologies.

### FR-2: Data Protection Features

*   The platform shall provide features for data protection, including data anonymization, encryption, and access control.
*   The platform shall allow users to set custom data protection policies and rules.
*   The platform shall provide real-time monitoring and alerts for data breaches and unauthorized access.

### FR-3: AI and Technology Integration

*   The platform shall provide integration with popular AI and technology frameworks, including machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision.
*   The platform shall allow users to easily integrate custom AI and technology models and algorithms.
*   The platform shall provide real-time monitoring and analytics for AI and technology performance.

### FR-4: User Management and Access Control

*   The platform shall provide user management and access control features, including user authentication, authorization, and role-based access control.
*   The platform shall allow administrators to set custom user roles and permissions.
*   The platform shall provide real-time monitoring and alerts for user activity and access.

### FR-5: Reporting and Analytics

*   The platform shall provide reporting and analytics features, including data visualization, dashboards, and custom reporting.
*   The platform shall allow users to set custom reporting and analytics requirements.
*   The platform shall provide real-time monitoring and alerts for reporting and analytics performance.

## Non-Functional Requirements
-----------------------------

### NFR-1: Performance

*   The platform shall respond to user input within 2 seconds.
*   The platform shall handle a minimum of 100 concurrent users without degradation.
*   The platform shall maintain a minimum uptime of 99.99%.

### NFR-2: Security

*   The platform shall implement end-to-end encryption for all data in transit and at rest.
*   The platform shall implement secure authentication and authorization protocols.
*   The platform shall maintain a minimum security rating of A- on the OWASP Top 10.

### NFR-3: Reliability

*   The platform shall maintain a minimum uptime of 99.99%.
*   The platform shall handle a minimum of 100 concurrent users without degradation.
*   The platform shall provide real-time monitoring and alerts for system performance and availability.

## Constraints
--------------

*   The platform shall be built using open-source technologies and frameworks.
*   The platform shall be deployable on a variety of cloud and on-premises infrastructure.
*   The platform shall be scalable to handle a minimum of 10,000 concurrent users.

## Assumptions
--------------

*   The platform shall be used by government officials, policymakers, and advocacy groups.
*   The platform shall be used for consent and data protection in AI and technology.
*   The platform shall be used in a variety of industries and use cases.
